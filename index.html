Seth's Five-Course Meal

Appetizer

Red Bell Pepper Soup

This delicious soup is perfect for starting the meal. It is made from roasted red bell peppers, vegetable broth, garlic, and cream. It is garnished with feta cheese and parsley leaves.

Salad

Kale Caesar Salad

The kale Caesar salad is made by tossing kale leaves with homemade Caesar dressing, breadcrumbs, and parmesan cheese. It is a tasty and healthy addition to any meal.

Entree

Baked chicken breast

The baked chicken breast is seasoned with a blend of spices that provide a savory and aromatic flavor. The chicken is seared and then baked in the oven with rosemary and thyme. It is served with a side of mashed potatoes and steamed vegetables.

Dessert

Oreo cheesecake chocolate

This Oreo cheesecake chocolate dessert is made with crushed Oreos, cream cheese, and melted chocolate. It is then chilled in the refrigerator to give a creamy texture.

Beverages

Pineapple Juice

To complete the meal, we have a glass of chilled pineapple juice. Pineapple juice is a good source of vitamin C and manganese. It is refreshing and perfect for quenching thirst.
